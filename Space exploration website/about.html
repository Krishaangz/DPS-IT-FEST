Our Involvement in DPS IT Fest 2024

SpaceByte is proud to be a part of the DPS IT Fest 2024, hosted by DPS Greater Faridabad. This prestigious event brings together young minds passionate about technology and space exploration.

Our team has worked tirelessly to create an immersive and educational platform that showcases the wonders of the universe. Through interactive elements, stunning visuals, and comprehensive information, we aim to inspire the next generation of space enthusiasts and scientists.

Our Mission

At SpaceByte, our mission is to make the complexities of space accessible to everyone. We believe that by fostering curiosity and providing engaging content, we can inspire more people to look up at the stars and dream big.

Our Team

We, Krishang Saharia and Aaradhy Tiwari, are passionate about space, technology, and education. Our team combines expertise in astrophysics, web development, and design to bring you this unique space exploration experience.
