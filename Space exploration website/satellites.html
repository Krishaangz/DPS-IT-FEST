Satellites

Satellites are human-made or natural objects that orbit planets, moons, or stars. They serve various purposes including communication, observation, navigation, and scientific research.

Categories of Satellites

Communication Satellites

These satellites help in transmitting communication signals like television, radio, and internet services.

Weather Satellites

Used for monitoring weather patterns and providing data for forecasting and climate analysis.

Navigation Satellites

Provide global positioning system (GPS) services for navigation and location tracking.

Earth Observation Satellites

These satellites monitor Earth's surface, climate, and other natural resources for environmental studies.

Scientific Satellites

Launched for the purpose of space exploration, research, and scientific experiments.

Military Satellites

Utilized for reconnaissance, surveillance, and communication for defense purposes.
