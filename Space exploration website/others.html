Other Discoveries in the Cosmos

The cosmos is a vast and mysterious realm filled with intriguing discoveries and theories. This page explores various aspects such as astrobiology, quantum mechanics, and more, revealing the complexities of our universe.

Key Theories and Discoveries

Astrobiology

The study of the origin, evolution, and potential existence of life in the universe.

Quantum Mechanics

A fundamental theory in physics describing the physical properties of nature at the scale of atoms and subatomic particles.

James Webb Space Telescope (JWST) Discoveries

Revolutionary findings from JWST that enhance our understanding of the universe, including the study of exoplanets and distant galaxies.

Pulsar Astronomy

The study of pulsars, which are rotating neutron stars that emit beams of radiation, providing insights into the behavior of matter under extreme conditions.

Space Debris and Mitigation

The challenge of managing and reducing the growing amount of debris in Earth's orbit, which poses risks to satellites and space missions.

Exoplanets

Planets located outside our solar system, which have been a focus of research in the search for extraterrestrial life.
