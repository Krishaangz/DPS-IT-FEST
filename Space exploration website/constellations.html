Zodiac Constellations

The zodiac constellations are a group of twelve star patterns that the sun passes through over the course of a year. These constellations have been used for millennia to track time and are tied to astrology.

Zodiac Constellations

Aries

Known as the Ram, this constellation is associated with leadership and new beginnings.

Taurus

Represented by the Bull, Taurus is connected with determination and reliability.

Gemini

The Twins symbolize duality and adaptability in this dynamic constellation.

Cancer

The Crab symbolizes emotional depth and intuition in this water sign constellation.

Leo

Known for its regal lion, Leo represents courage, strength, and leadership.

Virgo

The Virgin symbolizes purity, intelligence, and analytical skills in this earth sign.
