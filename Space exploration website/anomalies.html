Space Anomalies

Space anomalies refer to unusual occurrences in space that challenge our understanding of celestial phenomena. These anomalies can arise from various factors, including gravitational effects, magnetic fields, and cosmic events.

Types of Space Anomalies

Gravitational Anomalies

These anomalies occur due to unexpected gravitational influences that can affect the motion of celestial objects.

Magnetic Anomalies

Magnetic anomalies are variations in the Earth's magnetic field caused by irregularities in the planet's geology.

Temporal Anomalies

These anomalies involve irregularities in the passage of time, often theorized in relation to black holes or wormholes.

Cosmic Microwave Background Anomalies

Fluctuations in the cosmic microwave background radiation that challenge current cosmological models.

Dark Matter Anomalies

These anomalies relate to unexpected behavior of galaxies and galaxy clusters that suggest the presence of dark matter.

Stellar Anomalies

These anomalies involve unusual behaviors of stars, such as erratic brightness or peculiar spectral lines.
